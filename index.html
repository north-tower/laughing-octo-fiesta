Our History

For many years, Bethany has been honing her skills in interior design. In 2013, we proudly opened our first showroom in Brussels, Belgium, nestled on a charming street near the Grand Market. People from all corners of the globe were captivated by the impeccable craftsmanship, thoughtful designs, and luxurious aesthetics we offered. The showroom quickly flourished, becoming a resounding success in the world of interior design.
By 2015, our reputation had grown immensely, and demand soared. With no less than a hundred design projects undertaken each day, it was evident that we needed to expand. Bethany's Interior Design Shop moved into a larger, more prominent space, enabling us to accommodate the growing clientele and showcase our exceptional work.

Today, we are thrilled to invite you to experience the transformative power of our interior design services. Our team of highly skilled designers is dedicated to creating harmonious, captivating, and functional living spaces that reflect your unique vision and style. Whether you seek to revitalize a single room or embark on a comprehensive home renovation, Bethany's Interior Design Shop will collaborate with you every step of the way, translating your aspirations into reality.
